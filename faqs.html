Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about Aero SmartHub. Use the search bar or browse through our FAQs.

What is Aero SmartHub?
Aero SmartHub is a comprehensive platform designed to enhance airport experiences with features like flight tracking, analytics, and personalized assistance.
How does real-time flight tracking work?
We provide real-time flight tracking with data from multiple sources, allowing users to stay updated on flight schedules and statuses.
What services does Aero SmartHub offer?
We offer services like real-time flight tracking, personalized assistance, analytics dashboards, user profiles with rewards, and more.
How do I set up an account?
You can set up an account by clicking on the Sign Up button on our homepage and filling in the necessary details.
What browsers are supported?
Our platform supports modern browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge for an optimal user experience.
How is my data protected?
We prioritize user privacy and have implemented strict data security measures to protect your information.
