AERO SMART HUB

A passenger is an individual traveling on a vehicle or conveyance, such as an airplane, train, bus, or ship, but not in the capacity of a driver or operator. Passengers typically seek comfort and safety during their journey, and their experiences can be influenced by factors like amenities, services, and the efficiency of the transportation system. In the context of air travel, passengers are provided with a variety of services including check-in, baggage handling, in-flight entertainment, and refreshments.
